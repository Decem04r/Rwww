Jett - The Agile Duelist

Jett's unmatched mobility and agility make her a lethal duelist on the battlefield.

Abilities

Master Jett's abilities to outmaneuver and outplay opponents:

  • **Cloudburst**: Block vision with her quick smokes.
  • **Updraft**: Leap into unexpected positions.
  • **Tailwind**: Dash to reposition or escape.

Tips & Tricks

Become the ultimate duelist:

  • Use *Cloudburst* to block crucial sightlines.
  • Combine *Updraft* with surprise attacks.
  • Dash aggressively after a kill.
