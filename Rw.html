Jett - The Agile Duelist

Known for her mobility and agility, Jett dominates with her unpredictable movement and lethal precision.

Abilities

Master Jett's skills to outmaneuver your enemies:

  • Cloudburst: Block enemy vision with quick smokes.
  • Updraft: Gain vertical mobility to surprise enemies.
  • Tailwind: Dash quickly to reposition or escape.
  • Blade Storm: Deadly knives for precise takedowns.

Tips & Tricks

Master Jett with these strategies:

  • Use *Updraft* to gain unexpected high ground advantages.
  • Pair *Tailwind* with aggressive pushes or escapes.
  • Leverage *Cloudburst* to obscure enemy vision during critical moments.
  • Activate *Blade Storm* for clutch plays and precise eliminations.
