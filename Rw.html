Jett - The Agile Duelist

Jett is an agile duelist known for her high mobility, allowing her to outmaneuver opponents quickly. With her swift and unpredictable movement, she dominates in close combat, making her a lethal force on the battlefield.

Abilities

Cloudburst

Jett's Cloudburst is a versatile smoke ability that allows her to block vision and disrupt enemies.

Updraft

Updraft allows Jett to boost herself upward, offering a tactical advantage for high-ground plays.

Tailwind

Tailwind gives Jett a dash, enabling her to reposition quickly after getting a kill or moving through the map.

Ultimate: Jetstream

Jetstream enhances Jett's abilities to the extreme, boosting her mobility and giving her enhanced movement for a short period of time.

Tips and Tricks

  • Cloudburst is great for blocking vision during tight moments or escaping from dangerous situations.
  • Master Updraft to surprise enemies from above, and control high ground for tactical advantage.
  • Tailwind is perfect for repositioning and retreating, but also for aggressive pushes after taking down an enemy.
  • Jetstream lets you dominate a fight when used correctly. Don't waste it; make every dash count.
