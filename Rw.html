Jett - The Agile Duelist

Jett is known for her high mobility and fast-paced playstyle. She can dash around the map, making her one of the most agile agents in Valorant.

Abilities

  • Cloudburst - A smoke ability.
  • Updraft - A vertical boost to reposition quickly.
  • Tailwind - A dash that allows Jett to reposition in an instant.
  • Ultimate: Jetstream - A powerful movement boost with enhanced abilities.

Tips and Tricks

  • Use Cloudburst to block vision and take control of key positions.
  • Jett's dash can be used for quick escapes or aggressive pushes.
  • Mastering Updraft allows you to control vertical space and gain an advantage in high ground combat.
