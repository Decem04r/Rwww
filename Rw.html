Jett - The Agile Duelist

Jett is a high-mobility agent in *Valorant*, specializing in outmaneuvering opponents with speed and agility.

Abilities

Jett's toolkit includes smoke grenades, dashes, and an ultimate knife skill for eliminating enemies efficiently.

Tips and Tricks

Use Cloudburst to block vision, Tailwind for quick escapes, and master her ultimate for precise eliminations.
